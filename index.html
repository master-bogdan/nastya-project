About Me

Thank you for checking out my website. My name is Anastasiia Ellis. I’m an artist and a photographer located in Norfolk, Virginia. I love to take portraits of different people, capture their emotions and unique styles. I frequently experiment and try different styles. I’ll be overjoyed if you choose me to capture an important moment in your life! Don't worry if you struggle with posing or don’t know exactly what you want.

About Me

Thank you for visiting my website!

My name is Anastasiia Ellis. I’m an artist and a photographer located in Norfolk, Virginia. I love to capture emotions and unique styles through photography.

I understand that it is difficult for some people to relax in front of camera. I always start my photoshoots with a 10-15 min warmup so my clients can get more comfortable. I guide my clients, help with poses, and bring different props. You can choose your own location and style or I can suggest some.

I like to shoot earlier in the morning or in the evening to catch perfect light - if those time windows don't work for you, we can also come to my studio during your availability. I use Adobe Photoshop for skin retouching, body shaping and color adjustments, and finish with Adobe Lightroom.
